New Orleans Square

New Orleans Square is a themed land at Disneyland California that captures the charm, mystery, and festive atmosphere of 18th-century New Orleans. Designed with intricate wrought-iron balconies, cobblestone streets, gas lamps, and jazz music, this area immerses guests in the ambiance of Louisiana’s French Quarter. It’s a blend of southern elegance, pirate adventures, and supernatural tales, making it one of the most atmospheric lands in the park.

1. Pirates of the Caribbean

A slow boat ride that takes visitors through pirate-infested waters, telling the story of rebellious pirates with impressive animatronics, battle scenes, and treasure-laden caves. This attraction inspired the popular movie franchise.

2. Haunted Mansion

A spooky yet humorous dark ride through a ghost-filled manor. Guests ride in "Doom Buggies" and meet 999 happy haunts through scenes with supernatural effects and eerie music.

3. Live Jazz Performances

Live jazz performances in New Orleans Square at Disneyland California bring the vibrant spirit of New Orleans to life through upbeat music by bands like The Royal Street Bachelors and Jambalaya Jazz Band. Guests often stop to dance, clap, or simply enjoy the joyful rhythms, making the experience feel immersive and culturally rich.
