Fantasyland

Fantasyland is the heart of Disneyland’s classic storytelling charm. Inspired by beloved Disney fairy tales, this land brings animated stories to life through whimsical architecture, soft pastel colors, and iconic medieval European aesthetics. With Sleeping Beauty Castle as its enchanting gateway, Fantasyland immerses guests in a world of magic, fantasy, and nostalgia where dreams truly come true. It is especially beloved by families and young children, offering a wide variety of gentle rides and storybook adventures.

1. Peter Pan’s Flight

Guests "fly" over London and Neverland in a suspended pirate ship, following the classic story of Peter Pan and his battle with Captain Hook. The main station is located on Main Street and serves as the starting point for the journey around the park.

2. "It’s a Small World"

A gentle boat ride through scenes representing countries around the world, featuring singing dolls that promote peace and global unity.

3. Matterhorn Bobsleds

A high-speed roller coaster set inside a snowy mountain inspired by the Swiss Alps, complete with sudden turns and a roaring Yeti.

4. Mr. Toad’s Wild Ride

A dark ride through Mr. Toad’s chaotic adventures—racing through city streets, courtrooms, and even a whimsical version of "hell."

5. Snow White’s Enchanted Wish

This ride tells Snow White’s story through immersive scenes, from the enchanted forest to the Evil Queen’s lair, ending in a happily ever after.

6. Pinocchio’s Daring Journey

A fast-paced ride through key moments from Pinocchio’s tale, including Stromboli’s puppet show and the escape from Monstro the whale.

7. Alice in Wonderland

A whimsical ride into Wonderland, where guests encounter characters like the Cheshire Cat, the Queen of Hearts, and the Mad Hatter.
