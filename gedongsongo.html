Mickey's Toontown

**Mickey’s Toontown** at Disneyland California is a themed land designed to resemble the cartoon world where classic Disney characters like Mickey Mouse, Minnie, Goofy, Donald, and others live. With its quirky architecture, bright colors, and immersive atmosphere, it feels just like stepping into an animated film. A family favorite—especially for children—Toontown features explorable buildings, interactive elements, and the chance to meet iconic Disney characters in a safe, fun, and kid-friendly environment.

1. Mickey & Minnie’s Runaway Railway

A high-tech dark ride that takes guests into the animated world of Mickey and Minnie using trackless technology. It’s full of surprises, vibrant visuals, and cartoon-style adventures.

2. Roger Rabbit’s Car Toon Spin

An interactive dark ride where guests hop into a cartoon taxi and spin their own car while navigating through the chaotic city of Roger Rabbit.

3. Mickey’s House

Explore Mickey’s colorful home, filled with fun furniture and interactive items. At the end of the tour, guests can meet Mickey in person.

4. Minnie’s House

A cute and charming house filled with Minnie’s signature décor. Kids can interact with her kitchen and experience her cheerful world.

5. Goofy’s How-To-Play Yard

An outdoor play area themed around Goofy’s home, featuring safe and active physical play elements for younger children.

6. Donald’s Duck Pond

A light water play zone shaped like Donald’s boat, complete with sprays and interactive water features, perfect for little ones.

7. Chip ‘n Dale’s GADGETcoaster

A kid-friendly mini roller coaster themed after Gadget from Chip ‘n Dale Rescue Rangers — a great first coaster experience for young riders.
