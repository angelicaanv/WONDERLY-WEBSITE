Frontierland

Frontierland depicts the life and adventure of the American Wild West in the 19th century, with a cowboy and exploration theme as its main highlight. The area features red rock mountains, a man-made lake, and a Western-style town. One of its main attractions is Big Thunder Mountain Railroad, a roller coaster themed around mountain mining. Visitors are invited to explore the world of pioneers, gold miners, and Native American tribes. The design is filled with natural elements like wood, stone, and water, creating an authentic desert frontier atmosphere.

1. Big Thunder Mountain Railroad

A roller coaster themed around a gold mine in the mountains. One of Frontierland’s main icons, offering a fast and twisting ride through red rock canyons.

2. Mark Twain Riverboat

A classic 19th-century-style steamship that takes guests on a scenic journey along the Rivers of America. The surroundings include forest landscapes, animatronic wildlife, and Native American village scenes.

3. Pirate's Lair on Tom Sawyer Island

An adventure island accessible only by raft. Visitors can explore caves, swinging bridges, pirate hideouts, and the imaginative world of Tom Sawyer.

4. Disneyland Railroad – Frontierland Station

A Wild West-themed train station that is one of four stops on the Disneyland Railroad. It offers a relaxing ride around the entire park.

5. Frontierland Shootin' Exposition

An interactive shooting gallery where guests can use laser rifles to hit animatronic targets. The setting resembles an old Western town.
