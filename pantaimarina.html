Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge

Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge at Disneyland California is a fully immersive themed land that transports visitors to the remote outpost planet of Batuu in the Star Wars galaxy. Designed with extraordinary detail—from architecture and ambient sounds to smells and in-universe characters—this area makes guests feel like they’ve stepped right into a Star Wars movie. You’ll encounter characters like Rey, Chewbacca, Kylo Ren, and Stormtroopers as you explore markets, spacecraft hangars, and the famous Oga’s Cantina. With high-tech attractions and a rich, story-driven environment, Galaxy’s Edge is a must-visit for Star Wars fans of all ages.

1. Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run

Step aboard the iconic Millennium Falcon and go on an interactive smuggling mission. Guests take on roles as pilots, gunners, or engineers in this thrilling flight simulator featuring responsive controls and cinematic visuals.

2. Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance

One of Disneyland’s most technologically advanced rides, this epic attraction combines a trackless dark ride, simulator sequences, and lifelike animatronics. Guests join the Resistance and face off against the First Order in a large-scale, story-driven adventure.

3. Oga’s Cantina

A lively galactic bar offering themed drinks and snacks, all set to music spun by DJ R-3X, a reprogrammed droid. The cantina is known for its immersive setting and requires advance reservations due to popularity.

4. Droid Depot

Build your own custom droid that interacts with the environment. This hands-on experience is fun for all ages and allows you to take home a working, personalized Star Wars companion.

5. Savi’s Workshop – Handbuilt Lightsabers

An intimate and cinematic experience where guests construct a custom lightsaber guided by “Gatherers.” With storytelling and ceremonial touches, it’s a dream come true for fans who want to wield the power of the Force.

6. Merchant Row & Black Spire Outpost

A bustling marketplace filled with Star Wars-themed shops selling Resistance and First Order apparel, handmade galactic toys, Jedi gear, and collectibles universe souvenirs.
