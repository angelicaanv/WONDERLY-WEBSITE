Kota Lama

Main Street, USA is the first area that greets visitors upon entering Disneyland. Designed to resemble a small town in early 20th-century America, this area was inspired by Walt Disney’s hometown of Marceline, Missouri. The main street is lined with vintage-style buildings, classic hanging lamps, flags, and nostalgic ornaments that create a warm and memory-filled atmosphere.

1. Disneyland Railroad – Main Street Station

A classic steam train that circles the entire Disneyland park. The main station is located on Main Street and serves as the starting point for the journey around the park.

2. Great Moments with Mr. Lincoln

A theatrical show featuring an Audio-Animatronic figure of Abraham Lincoln. It tells the story of American history with dramatic visuals and sound.

3. The Disneyland Story presenting Walt Disney

A small exhibition area that tells the story of Walt Disney and the creation of Disneyland. It is often combined with the Mr. Lincoln attraction.

4. Horse-Drawn Streetcars

Streetcars pulled by horses, giving visitors a ride down Main Street.

5. Omnibus & Jitney

Vintage vehicles such as the double-decker Omnibus and the smaller Jitney car that visitors can ride to explore Main Street.

6. Main Street Cinema

A classic-style cinema on Main Street that screens vintage Mickey Mouse and Disney short films in black and white.

7. Dapper Dans

A barbershop quartet that performs live musical shows along Main Street, adding charm and harmony to the atmosphere.
